Dan Carlo Jontilano

THE MAIN 3

Tanjiro Kamado

A kind-hearted demon slayer driven by love for his sister. Uses Water Breathing and the rare Sun Breathing technique.

Zenitsu Agatsuma

Fearful and anxious, but becomes unstoppable when unconscious. Masters Thunder Breathing with incredible speed.

Inosuke Hashibira

Raised in the mountains, fights with instinct and aggression. Uses Beast Breathing and dual swords.

THE 9 HASHIRA

Gyomei Himejima

The Stone Hashira, known for unmatched strength and compassion. Uses powerful stone-based breathing.

Kyojuro Rengoku

Flame Hashira with fiery passion and unwavering spirit. Fights to protect others and uphold justice.

Sanemi Shinazugawa

A brutal and stubborn Wind Hashira who hides his kindness behind anger. Fierce in battle with strong instincts.

Giyu Tomioka

Quiet and serious Water Hashira. Calm in battle and highly skilled in precision and defense.

Tengen Uzui

The flashy Sound Hashira who loves extravagance. Uses sound-based attacks and explosive speed.

Mitsuri Kanroji

The Love Hashira known for her warmth and strength. Uses flexible sword techniques and superhuman speed.

Muichiro Tokito

The Mist Hashira who is calm and distant. A prodigy with incredible reflexes and mist-based attacks.

Obanai Iguro

The Snake Hashira, strict and disciplined. Uses precise slithering sword movements and deadly techniques.

Shinobu Kocho

The Insect Hashira who fights with speed and poison. Uses medical knowledge and lethal stings.

THE UPPER MOONS

Upper Moon One – Kokushibo

Former demon slayer turned strongest Upper Moon. Uses Moon Breathing and overwhelming sword power.

Upper Moon Two – Doma

Cold and charismatic demon who uses ice and freezing techniques. Views humans as weak and meaningless.

Upper Moon Three – Akaza

A brutal fighter obsessed with strength. Uses Destructive Death martial arts and fierce speed.

Upper Moon Four – Hantengu

Divides into multiple demons based on emotions. Each form has unique abilities and strength.

Upper Moon Five – Gyokko

A demon artist who uses water pottery and monsters. Known for his twisted creativity and cruelty.

Upper Moon Six – Gyutaro & Daki

Siblings who share the Upper Moon rank. Fight together with deadly teamwork and blood demon arts.
